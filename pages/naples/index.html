Napoli

Naples, Italy

March 19th, 2017

After working at the restaurant for 3 months, I sat down to have lunch in the tavern, the more casual of the restaurant's two dining rooms.

To start, they sent us each a chilled and roasted oyster, which I particularly appreciated since I had been shucking oysters for the past 3 months. The chilled oyster is topped with grilled leek mignonette and the roasted oyster is with pickled burdock and black trumpet mushrooms.

Next we had the soup from the soup and sandwich combo. The soup was a pumpkin cheese soup with croutons, chili oil.

The Gramercy Tavern burger is one of the best I've ever had. It's topped with a blend of cabot clothbound cheddar and Alpha Tolman Gruyere, and served with pickled fresno peppers and potato chips fried in duck fat. The burger itself is a blend of short rib brisket and chuck.

The sandwich, which is an off menu special, had smoked and roasted pork belly, salsa verde, avocado, and caraflex cabbage. Served with a beautiful side salad of gem lettuce and baby kale.

Finally, the deep dish apple buckwheat pie for dessert. Served with a caramel apple sauce and cheddar ice cream. We left feeling so full!
